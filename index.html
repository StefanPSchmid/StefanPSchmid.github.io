About me

I am an incoming PhD researcher to the Digital Chemistry Laboratory at ETH Zurich. Prior, I studied chemistry for a BSc and MSc at ETH Zurich as a fellow of the Excellence Scholarship and Opportunity Programme. My Master's degree included a stay as a Visiting Student in the MatterLab at the University of Toronto.

My Research

During my Master studies at ETH Zurich, I specialized on EPR and NMR spectroscopic studies of heterogeneous catalytic systems. During a stay as visiting student in the MatterLab, I focused on the application of Machine Learning algorithms to study chemical reactivity.
